About Us

Christian Fellowship is a Discord server run by many great staff members:

  • DeeDeeAich - Owner
  • R.A. Miller - Administrator
  • Royale - Administrator
  • Timmy - Administrator
  • Snickerdoodles - Moderator
  • Smartcookie - Moderator
  • Loving Life - Trial-Moderator
Here, Christians can have fellowship together. We have in-depth, theological discussions where the Gospel is preached. We allow for anyone to join (Atheists, Muslims, etc) and allow them to debate, discuss, and understand the Christian faith. We pray that this server may be edifying and uplifting to all who join and that all will come to a true, saving faith in the Lord Jesus Christ. We are centered around God's word and offer Bible Studies, prayer and reading nights, and so much more. Come and join today!
